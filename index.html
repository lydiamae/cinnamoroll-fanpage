Cinnamoroll

Cinnamoroll is a Sanrio character. He is a long-eared white puppy with rosy cheeks and a tail that looks like a cinnamon roll.

    Things to know about Cinnamoroll:
  • He can fly!
  • He was born on a cloud
  • He is the second most successful Sanrio character, after Hello Kitty.
